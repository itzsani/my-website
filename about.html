About Me

Hello! I'm Syed Hussain-Sani, a recent graduate from the New Jersey Institute of Technology with a degree in Computer Information Systems. I have a passion for technology, especially in areas like web development, data analysis, and IT support. My goal is to continuously grow my skills and contribute to meaningful projects that leverage technology to solve real-world problems.

During my studies, I gained practical experience through various internships and projects. I worked as an IT Support Specialist at Bloomfield Board of Education, where I honed my skills in troubleshooting, software installation, and user support. Additionally, my internship at Kornit Digital provided me with hands-on experience in operations and logistics, giving me a well-rounded understanding of how technology supports business processes.

I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges. Whether it's coding a new application, analyzing complex datasets, or finding creative solutions to technical problems, I'm committed to delivering high-quality results and continuously improving my skillset.
